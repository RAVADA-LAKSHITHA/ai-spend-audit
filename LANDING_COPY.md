## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**
A: Yes, completely free. No credit card, no trial period, no account required. You enter your tools, get your audit, see your results. We ask for your email after you see results — only if you want the report delivered. The tool exists because Credex (our parent company) sells discounted AI credits, and audits help surface people who could save more through Credex. If your savings are significant, we may suggest a Credex consultation. That is the business model — transparent and optional.

**Q: How current is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine traces back to the official vendor pricing page, verified during the submission week of this project. We document the source URL and date for every number in PRICING_DATA.md. AI tool pricing changes frequently — we commit to monthly verification. If you spot an outdated price, email us and we will fix it within 24 hours.

**Q: Does the tool store my data?**
A: The audit inputs (tools, plans, spend amounts, team size) are stored to generate your shareable report URL. We never store or display your email address in public-facing pages. The shareable URL shows tools and savings numbers only — your company name and email are stripped from the public version.

**Q: Can I share my audit results?**
A: Yes — every audit generates a unique public URL with Open Graph previews for clean link sharing on Twitter, LinkedIn, and Slack. Identifying details are stripped from the public version. Only tools and savings numbers are shown.

**Q: What if my AI tool isn't listed?**
A: We currently support Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, Gemini, Windsurf, and Perplexity AI. These cover the majority of AI tool spend for developer teams. If you use a tool not listed, add it as a custom entry with your monthly spend and we will include it in the total. We are adding more tools based on user requests.
